DS Send Email Payslips

The module helps to attatch payslip with E-mail send to Employees

Send By Email Button in payslip view

Select an employee payslip and then Click button "Send By Email"

Email template with attached payslip

Send By Email Action in Employee Payslips list view

Select list of the Employee Payslips and then Choose Action "Send Email Payslips"

Message confirm Email Payslips is successfully sent

Send By Email Button in Payslips Batches form view

Select an Payslips Batches and then Click button "Send Email Payslips"

Message confirm to send the Email Payslips to all Employess in this Payslips Batches

Message confirm Email Payslips is successfully sent
